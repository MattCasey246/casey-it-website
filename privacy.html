Privacy Notice

How Casey IT Limited collects, uses, and protects your personal data.

Casey IT Limited · casey-it.co.uk · Last updated: 18 April 2026

Who we are

Casey IT Limited is a company registered in England and Wales (Company No. 12074186), with Matt Casey as Director and data controller for personal data collected on this website.

If you have any questions about how we handle your data, contact us at: info@casey-it.co.uk

What we collect

We only collect personal data when you choose to contact us via the enquiry form on our Contact page. The data collected is:

  • Full name (required)
  • Email address (required)
  • Message / project description (required)
  • Company or organisation name (optional)
  • Phone number (optional)
  • How you found us (optional)

We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising, and we do not run any analytics on this website. Google Fonts are loaded to render the site correctly; this may involve a request to Google's servers but no personal data is retained by us as a result.

Why we collect it and our lawful basis

We collect your contact details to respond to your enquiry and, where relevant, to discuss potential projects or engagements with you.

The lawful basis for this processing is legitimate interests (UK GDPR Article 6(1)(f)) — specifically, our legitimate interest in responding to business enquiries made to us. We only use your data for the purpose for which you contacted us.

Who we share your data with

We use Formspree to receive and forward enquiry form submissions to us by email. Formspree acts as a data processor on our behalf and handles your submission data in accordance with their own privacy policy, which you can review at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any other third parties. We will not use your details to send you unsolicited marketing without your consent.

How long we keep your data

We retain enquiry correspondence for as long as is reasonably necessary to handle your enquiry and any resulting engagement. If no engagement follows, we will not retain your data beyond the period needed to close the enquiry. You can request deletion at any time — see Your rights below.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law (UK GDPR / Data Protection Act 2018), you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure — request that we delete your personal data
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we process your data in certain circumstances
  • Portability — request your data in a portable, machine-readable format
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@casey-it.co.uk. We will respond within one month.

Your right to complain

If you are unhappy with how we have handled your personal data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK's supervisory authority:

Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)
Website: ico.org.uk
Telephone: 0303 123 1113

We would appreciate the opportunity to resolve any concern directly — please email us first at info@casey-it.co.uk.

Changes to this notice
